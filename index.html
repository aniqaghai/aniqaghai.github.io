About Me

Hello, I'm Anika Ghai, a high school student with a deep passion for both the arts and sciences. Based in the vibrant city of Lucknow, I am currently pursuing my studies in Mathematics, Physics, and Design and Technology.

These subjects not only challenge my analytical skills but also align perfectly with my lifelong passion for architecture. I believe that understanding the principles of design and the mechanics of structures will provide a strong foundation for my future in the field of architecture.

My goal is to create innovative and sustainable architectural designs that blend functionality with aesthetic beauty, contributing to the development of smarter, more efficient living spaces.
